SASE LSU

WELCOME TO SASE LSU!

The Society of Asian Scientists & Engineers (SASE) aims to help scientists, engineers, and technologists reach their full career potential through professional development, cultural awareness, and giving back to their community. Here, you can learn more about the SASE Drexel Chapter and all that we do. We look forward to having you join our SASE Family!

OUR THREE PILLARS

Professional Development

Did you know that SASE members have received co-op opportunities through SASE? Being a SASE member has many benefits that can line you up for your next job! In addition, SASE hosts workshops to develop leadership and professional skills. You can learn interview skills, have your resume reviewed, establish technical skills, and much more.

Community Service

Do good and feel good while helping those in need. Making contributions to our local community is an important aspect of SASE. Being a good leader involves helping those around you, and we uphold this philosophy by volunteering and participating in community service events around the city.

Celebrating Diversity

Explore different cultures throughout SASE. Whether it be on campus or in the workplace, we encourage an environment where we can learn from people from all different backgrounds.
